Factory farming is an industrial process in which animals and the products they generate are mass produced. Factory farming has seen a big spike in last 25 years. With the immense population growth, the demand for white and red meat has been on the rise. 99% of Farm animals in the United Sates are raised on factory farms.

Because factory farming is a business, ultimate goal is to maximize production and as a result make a profit. The animals on these farms are not seen individual, sentient beings with unique physical and psychological needs but as something that can be bred, fed, confined, and drugged to lay more eggs, birth more offspring, and die with more meat on their bones.
